FAQ

Do I need to be a member?

No. Lessons are available to both members and non-members at Kings Norton Golf Club.

What should I bring?

Your clubs, a glove if you use one. Golf balls and training aids are all provided. Clubs can be hired out if required and available in right and left handed.

Cancellation policy

Please provide at least 24 hours' notice. Late cancellations and no shows may be charged at my discretion.

Do you coach juniors?

Yes. Junior coaching is available from the age of 5 and upwards, for all abilities.
